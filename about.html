Book

‘GIS and Data Visualisation using QGIS and R Studio’ is an educational book designed to introduce QGIS and R Studio to anyone with an interest in mapping and data visualisation. It also discusses the basic principles of marine and climate science using openly available atmospheric carbon-dioxide, sea surface temperature, hurricane track data and ecosystem shapefiles (e.g. coral reefs).
